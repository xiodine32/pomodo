About PomoDO

  • PomoDO uses the Pomodoro© technique to boost your productivity!

  • PomoDO announces when you need to take a break or start working again by simple sounds, in a retro 8bit style.

  • PomoDO's easy to use interface is extremely simple, you can start working with just a simple click!

  • PomoDO automatically starts your next break or work session! No need to manually press the start button!

  • Need to take an interruption from your PomoDO? Just click on the timer!

  • Should you want to end your current PomoDO, use the end button! It's as simple as it gets!
